## About Us
Great Question's all-in-one UX research platform combines a research CRM, mixed method tools, and a research repository with enterprise-grade security and powerful AI features. Trusted by user-centric companies like Canva, Miro, and Gusto, Great Question makes it easy to:
- Recruit and screen participants, automate scheduling and incentive distribution, and grow your research panel.
- Conduct research studies, from user interviews, focus groups, and surveys, to prototype tests, card sorts, and tree tests.
- Store recordings, transcripts, reports, highlights, and reels in a secure repository that's SOC2, GDPR, and HIPAA compliant.
- Synthesize research data with advanced AI features and easily share what you learn with your team.
By consolidating the entire research process in one place, Great Question helps teams streamline research operations, reduce overhead, and democratize research, safely and at scale.
